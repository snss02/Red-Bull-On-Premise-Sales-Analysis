# Red Bull On-Premise Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Red Bull On-Premise Sales virtual experience program. The objective was to analyze historical sales data from different on-premise accounts and generate business insights that could help improve future sales performance.

The dataset included account-level sales information from 2017 to 2021 across multiple customer types such as Bars, Restaurants, Clubs, and Hotels/Event Venues.

Using Excel, I analyzed trends, account performance, product assortment impact, and marketing program effectiveness to understand what drives growth.

---

## Business Objective

To identify:

- Which account types are growing the fastest  
- Best and worst performing customer accounts  
- Impact of promotions and visibility programs on sales  
- Whether wider product assortment improves performance  
- Opportunities to improve declining accounts  

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables  
- Pivot Charts

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Sales Growth by Account Type

Compared total sales growth from 2017 to 2021 across:

- Bars  
- Clubs  
- Hotels / Event Venues  
- Restaurants  

**Insight:** Clubs showed the strongest growth, while Bars grew at a slower pace.

---

### 2. Best and Worst Performing Accounts

Used CAGR to identify top and bottom performing accounts in each category.

**Insight:** High-performing accounts often had stronger promotions and wider product availability.

---

### 3. Product Assortment Impact

Analyzed the effect of offering:

- Regular  
- Sugar Free  
- Yellow Edition  

**Insight:** Accounts with broader assortment generally achieved higher sales.

---

### 4. Marketing & Promotion Effectiveness

Measured the relationship between sales and promotional programs such as:

- Cooler Placement  
- Digital Screens  
- Menu Inclusion  
- Posters  

**Insight:** Menu inclusion and digital visibility were strongly linked with better sales performance.

---

## Key Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-growth channels like Clubs and Restaurants  
- Improve weak-performing Bar accounts with stronger promotions  
- Expand product assortment in declining locations  
- Replicate successful strategies from top-performing accounts  
- Use menu inclusion and branded visibility to drive demand
